The II English Teaching Congress is intended for English educators of Huetar Northern Region. This region includes Guatuso County, Los Chiles County, San Carlos County, and Upala County, district Rio Cuarto (Grecia), district San Isidro de Peñas Blancas (San Ramón) of Alajuela; and the districts of Puerto Viejo and La Virgen, Sarapiquí County of Heredia.
