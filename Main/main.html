English is the most widely spoken language in the world, which makes it an essential tool for the development of competencies in socio-economic and educational contexts. The II English Teaching Congress, Huetar Northern Region, Costa Rica seeks to offer an opportunity to enhance pedagogical experiences through the dialogue in terms of the challenges that educators face in the Huetar Northern Region. The main objective is to contribute to the improvement of teaching practices.

This executive summary presents the mission and vision of the congress. It also indicates the objectives, the importance, the location, the main theme and the thematic strands. Finally, it states the target population, the dates of the congress, and the Organizing Committee. The main purpose of this summary is to provide relevant information about the congress.


To contribute with the improvement of the teaching practices of English educators of Huetar Northern Region, through the development and exchanging of pedagogical experiences, effective training, and the use of innovative tools of the teaching-learning process of English as a foreign language.


To promote academic spaces to delve into pedagogical meditation, collaboration, and effective feedback to contribute to the effectiveness of the teaching-learning process of English as a foreign language of Huetar Northern´s English educators.


  • To strengthen the educational opportunities for the development of innovative teaching practices and pedagogical mediation of English teaching as a foreign language through the establishment of collaborative partnerships in order to contribute with the socio-economical, educational, and cultural development of Región Huetar Norte, Costa Rica.
  • Provide innovative pedagogical tools to the English teachers of Huetar Northern Region, Costa Rica to strengthen the teaching-learning process of English as a foreign language.
  • Establish collaboration, networking, and academic exchange among the English teachers and the educational institutions of Huetar Northern Region, Costa Rica.
  • Promote strategic partnerships between educational institutions of Huetar Northern Region, Costa Rica to strengthen the teaching-learning process of English as a foreign language.


The II English Teaching Congress is intended for English educators of Huetar Northern Region. This region includes Guatuso County, Los Chiles County, San Carlos County, and Upala County, district Rio Cuarto (Grecia), district San Isidro de Peñas Blancas (San Ramón) of Alajuela; and the districts of Puerto Viejo and La Virgen, Sarapiquí County of Heredia.


The II English Teaching Congress aims at providing the English teachers of Región Huetar Norte, Costa Rica with innovative pedagogical practices when teaching English as a second language. The congress seeks to promote a reflective and interactive academic space in order to create a positive socio-academic impact inside and outside the classroom.


    • Macro skills (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing).
    • Micro skills (Grammar, Vocabulary, Pronunciation).
    • Technology applied to the teaching of English as a foreign language.
    • Successful experiences in the teaching of English as a foreign language.
    • Creating testing and assessment tools.
    • Evaluation of competences.
    • Diagnostic, formative, and summative assessment.
